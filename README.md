# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes retail transaction data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and sales distribution between countries.

## Goal
Understand how revenue changes over time, which products contribute the most to sales, and which are top 10 countries to generate the highest revenue.

## Data
- Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/carrie1/ecommerce-data
- Files used:
  - data.csv

## Methodology
- Data cleaning and preparation
- Revenue calculation
- Grouping
- Comparison 

## Results
- Monthly revenues were calculated
- Top 10 products with highest revenue were highlighted
- Countries revenue concentration was measured
- The United Kingdom is the dominant market here
- A second comparison excludes UK for 'around-same' revenue for each country
